FAQ

Got questions?

How does StudyPal generate flashcards? +
StudyPal uses large language models to read your uploaded content and extract key concepts, definitions, and relationships. It then formats these into clean, concise flashcard pairs automatically. The whole process takes under 30 seconds for most documents.
What file types can I upload? +
StudyPal supports PDF, Word (.docx), PowerPoint (.pptx), plain text, and Google Docs links. We also support YouTube video URLs — StudyPal will transcribe and study from the lecture for you.
Is my data private and secure? +
Absolutely. Your notes and uploaded files are encrypted at rest and in transit. We never use your content to train our AI models, and you can delete all your data at any time from your account settings.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, completely. Cancel anytime from your account settings with no questions asked. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period, and we won't charge you again.
Is there a student discount? +
StudyPal Pro at $9/month is already our student price! If you're part of a university program, reach out to us — we offer institutional licensing for departments and student organizations.
